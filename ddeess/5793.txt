When high-powered attorney Collier Smith finds a bruised Brittany Banks on a rainy Tennessee mountain road, he saves her life and changes his own. Collier is on the fast track to a federal judgeship and engaged to a socialite. But Brittany, a beauty from the wrong side of the tracks, reminds him of the past he struggles to forget. Now Collier longs to protect and possess her--a need that drives him deeper into her world. (May)
