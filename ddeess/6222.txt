Robin Lear is living a jet-set life--until the day her father tells her he's dying. He further shatters her illusions by demoting her from the cushy job in the family business to teach her "about life." Jake Manning has had some tough knocks in life. Now all he wants is to make enough money to finish his degree and maybe get a nice house with a nice wife and kid. But this Prada-clad ice princess is turning his life upside down with her sassy--and disastrous--ways.
