New York Times Bestselling AuthorAnne Silver's brother had been working to expose a clever ring of swindlers when he was struck down by a car -- a clear case of attempted murder. Now Anne's determined to finish what her brother started. But she can't do it without the help of ex-CIA agent Julian Aries, a man who once betrayed her. With Julian as an unwilling conspirator, the couple goes undercover to face ghosts old and new -- an unsolved murder and a clever gang of fake psychics. But Anne and Julian put there lives on the line, the chill of danger and desire they can no longer deny force them to confront the greatest unsolved mystery of all . . . their unlikely love.
