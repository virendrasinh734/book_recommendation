When funnyman and "Miami Herald" columnist Dave Barry asked readers about their least favorite tunes, the witty writer was flabbergasted by the response: more than 10,000 readers voted! Based on the results of this monumental survey, Barry has compiled a book filled with the lyrics of some of the worst songs ever written, including such special categories as "Teen Death Songs", "Songs That People Always Get Wrong", and, of course, "Weenie Music".
