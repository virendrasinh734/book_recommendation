Dennis Hensley, the author of the best-selling Misadventures in the (213) has been getting together with a group of his friends for years to watch and crack wise about such films as Taxi Driver, Pretty Woman, A Star is Born, Armageddon, Saturday Night Fever, Basic Instinct and many more. Affectionately scathing, hilarious and revealing, this fresh and funny look at everyone's favourite movies simultaneously celebrates and puts them in their place.
