In this poignant sequel to "Cold Tea on a Hot Day, " Marilee James and Tate Holloway are engaged, but Marilee doesn't expect to deal with her ex-husband and her son's father, Stuart, who suddenly returns to Valentine, Oklahoma, after two years. Marilee is soon caught in a tug-of-war between Stuart and Tate--all of which forces her to question her ability to choose a compatible mate. (July)
