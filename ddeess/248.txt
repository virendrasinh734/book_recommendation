#1 New York Times bestselling author Nora Roberts presents three of her favorite Irish stories—together in one volume for the first time. SPELLBOUND A bewitchingly beautiful lady casts a thousand-year love spell on the man of her dreams—and unleashes a nightmare that only true love can conquer... EVER AFTER An ancient star pendant sends an enchanting woman to an otherwordly land—where she introduces a skeptical stranger to the magical powers of love... IN DREAMS A beautiful young woman is drawn to a castle in the forests of Ireland and becomes the link to a stranger’s past—and the curse that has trapped him forever in the eternity of his own dreams... "Spellbound" previously appeared in Once Upon a Castle "Ever After" previously appeared in Once Upon a Star "In Dreams" previously appeared in Once Upon a Dream
