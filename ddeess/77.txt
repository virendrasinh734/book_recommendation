How do we find our deep spiritual connection with life? And if we find it, how do we keep it? How do we engage in the daily tangle of practical affairs, yet still see and reach through to this mysterious connection? What kind of goal would make this possible?At the age of forty, the author casts away a lifetime of preconceptions to wrestle once more with these fundamental questions. His enquiry brings him to an extraordinary decision that will literally change his view of the world forever.When no value is found in a book without covers it is thrown away, only to find its way, still, to the one who needs it most. A book without covers becomes a companion in grief and a gateway to limitless possibility...
