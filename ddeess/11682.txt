Determined to escape an arranged marriage, oil heiress Evelyn Thurgood-Baron poses as a maid and hops the next train out of Beaumont, Texas. New Orleans policeman Luke Devereaux, framed and branded a criminal, will do anything to clear his name--even wear women's clothes. When Evelyn discovers her "employer's" true identity, she stands by this man who can offer her nothing but adventure--and unbridled passion.
