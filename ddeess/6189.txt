Despite their families' bitter feud, Roque Blackstone and Ritz Keller fall in love, sharing one night of passion although their love can have no future. With the loss of her family's respect, the life of her unborn child, and a loveless marriage to another man, Ritz is forced to admit ten years later she still loves Roque. But will the tragedies of their past deny them the future they are destined to share?
