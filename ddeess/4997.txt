Attorney Frank O'Connell's need to live on the edge cost him his family and a partnership in his father-in-law's D.C. firm. Now, he combs the cell blocks for clients. Then Ashley Bronson walks into his life, accused of the murder of a former cabinet official that has propelled her from the society column to the front page. And she wants Frank to defend her.
