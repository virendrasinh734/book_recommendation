Every now and then--between the car pools, bake sales, and mortgage payments--suburban wife and mother Emily Hudson stops to catch her breath. She thinks of James, her first love, and how, before life became complicated, she was Jimmy's girl. One day, thirty years after she and Jimmy swore their undying love for each other as teenagers, Emily musters up enough courage to find him--and to her delight, he's just as curious as she is. Now, in one unforgettable weekend, they will learn what went wrong between them--and find out if first love deserves a second chance. --back cover.
