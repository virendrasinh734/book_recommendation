Media consultant Veronica St. John is asked by the government to teach a rugged Navy SEAL to impersonate a European prince who's been targeted by terrorists. When she meets Lieutenant Joe Catalanotto, everything about him says regular guy--not royalty. As impossible as the task seems, Veronica's biggest problem with "Prince Joe" is the way he makes her want to slap his arrogant face--then kiss him senseless. (May)
