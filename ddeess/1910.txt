When Julianna Starr selects Kate and Richard Ryan to be the parents of her unborn child, she opens the door to a nightmare. Julianna sees Richard as her fantasy man and begins to stalk the couple. She molds herself in Kate's image and insinuates herself into Richard's life, determined to tear their marriage apart.
