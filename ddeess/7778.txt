Despite Federation delegate Lwaxana Troi's romantic overtures toward Captain Picard, a Tizarin wedding celebration aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise seems to go smoothly until the situation is complicated by the arrival of the notorious and all powerful being called Q -- who has come to examine and challenge the human concept of love. Suddenly, the festivities are in turmoil, the powerful Tizarin families are on the verge of war, and Lwaxana Troi is determined to teach Q a lesson in love that he will never forget.
