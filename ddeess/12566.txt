To join a popular gang at school, 11-year-old Robin is told he must kidnap old Mrs. Nordling's prize-winning cat. Sneaking into the Nordlings' house, Robin hears an argument and then sees Mr. Nordling covered in his wife's blood. Grabbing the cat, Robin runs for his life. Mr. Nordling knows someone besides the cat witnessed the brutal crime and will stop at nothing to learn who it is. Martin's Press.
