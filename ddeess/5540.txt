This masterfully written work of fiction by the bestselling author of The Heiress, A Holiday of Love, Remembrance, The Invitation, and Sweet Liar tells the story of Kady Ling, a celebrated chef about to married to a drop-dead gorgeous man. But an antique satin wedding dress first makes her dizzy and then transports her to 19th-century Colorado, where she winds up tricked into marrying a stranger to keep him from being hanged.
