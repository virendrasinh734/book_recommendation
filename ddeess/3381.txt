When Bobby and Susan Martin find a dirty, shivering child at their campsite, they don't expect to be drawn into an unthinkable crime. But when one of the boy's kidnappers comes out of the brush waving a gun, Bobby is forced to react, and the predator is brutally murdered. But was he a criminal--or a cop? With a vicious crime ring closing in on them, Bobby and Susan plunge into the heart of danger to save the boy--and themselves.
