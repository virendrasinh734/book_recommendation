Sarah Quilliam is trying to buy back the Inn at Hemlock Falls, New York. Her sister Meg keeps the patrons happy with her culinary abilities. They are delighted when they learn that a wealthy Finn wants to invest in the Inn. However, the sisters have been quarrelling nonstop. So when a group of TV writers checks in and the show's producer, which is far from jolly, is found dead, things change. The Finn doesn't think there's anything funny about murder.
