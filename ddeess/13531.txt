Wolverine, Cyclops, Jean Grey, Storm, and Rogue, mutants born with remarkable powers, band together as the X-Men to protect a world that fears and hates them from the powerful mutant Magneto. Original. (A 20th Century Fox film, written by Ed Solomon & Christopher McQuarrie, produced by Lauren Shuler Donner & Ralph Winter, directed by Bryan Singer, starring Patrick Stewart, Ian McKellen, James Marsden, Famke Janssen, Halle Berry, Anna Paquin, Rebecca Romijn-Stamos, Ray Park, & Hugh Jackman) (Science Fiction & Fantasy)
