The first title in a new mystery series. Widowed at age 52, Lynne Montgomery rediscovers her love for traveling. When Lynne and a tour group arrive in the village of Villa Belladuce in the Sonoma Wine Valley, murder adds itself to the itinerary. When Lynne discovers the body of the Villa's former owner, she finds there is plenty of bottled-up bitterness for the departed vintner. (May)
