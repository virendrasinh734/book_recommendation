When Ruby Runyon's date turns out to be mob-connected and married, she crashes a swank party to escape and meets Keaton Hamilton Danning III. He's out of her league, but very protective. Keaton has a secret: he's chief adviser to one of the world's most snobbish royals and longs to break free from that society. He finds Ruby sexy and sassy. But is he willing to take all she has to offer--including her love?
