Within a few weeks, Michael Fallon's life has unraveled. The uncle who raised him and his fiancee have been murdered, and he has been nearly killed by muggers himself. Then Fallon is fired by the investment firm where he has been employed and warned not to ask questions. Leaving New York, he attempts to rebuild his life on Martha's Vineyard--unaware that the powerful counterfeit drug ring that destroyed his life is tracking him.
