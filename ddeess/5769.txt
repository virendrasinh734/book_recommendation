A perfect companion for 'Take Time for your Life', Cheryl Richardson now applies her renowned coaching know-how to show readers how making small changes over time can have a huge impact. In fifty-two simple yet profound essays, she provides a weekly plan for both reflecting and taking action, along with specific, practical advice and exercises to help readers gradually and permanently remodel their lives, week by week. Easy to use and fun to read, these workbook topics include: The gift of Time: Self-management, not time management. Are we having fun yet? When life gets too serious, here's what to do. Give your brain a break: For the best ideas, try this. Standing in the shadow: Whose talent are you hiding behind? Stop juggling and start living: Here are some of the balls to drop. Close encounters: How can you make a deeper connection with others.
