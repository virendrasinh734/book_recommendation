With this collection of ancient spells for modern times, Athena Starwoman and Deborah Gray have created the world's first interactive Magic book. The powerful spells encompass: Love and sex spells -- a love goddess must have the right bedroom Naughty spells for naughty girls -- don't get mad, get even Money and success spells -- money will come when you're ready to receive it Me, me and I spells -- what you think, you become Home and family spells -- love will come in many forms The rest of your life spells -- you create your own destiny As the authors say, believe in yourself and in the absolute power of love. And remember, the best witches always look fabulous!
