When globe-hopping British First Secretary Peter Darwin returns to his childhood home fo a visit, he is sorry. Waiting for him were long-hidden memories, a string of racehorse deaths and homicide. As Darwin begins to realize that the key to all of it involves his own past, he wishes he'd never come back, because he just might never leave again --- alive.... "One of Francis' most winning novels." SAN FRANCISCO CHRONICLE Selected by the Book-of-the-Month Club
