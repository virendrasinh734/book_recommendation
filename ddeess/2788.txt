The Dolphin is a story of courage, of struggle against our own fears, our own limits. It reminds us that there is more to life than what meets the eye: things we can only discover if we follow our own rules. It is a story of hope that unveils the magic of this world, the magic we too often seem to forget. Follow your dreams, listen to the place within yourself, and let Daniel Dolphin take you through the most magical journey of all - the quest to fulfill your destiny.
