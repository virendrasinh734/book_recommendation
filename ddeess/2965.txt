Ruby, the rabbi's widow in Eternal, Texas, is back for her third mystery. Hoping to boost the new rabbi's approval rating at Temple Rita, busybody Essie Sue Margolis organizes a group vacation on a bargain cruise line to the Caribbean. Ruby fears paradise at these prices may be a hoax. Soon, the ship's lecturer is dead. The good news is Ruby discovers love on deck. The bad news is he may be a killer. Original.
