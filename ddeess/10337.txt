Pretty, blond child-star Lisa Whelchel was cast as a snobby rich girl on the wildly popular eighties sitcom The Facts of Life, which is still delighting fans worldwide. But who is the real woman behind the actress? And how did she become a voice for faith and family life today? How did Lisa escape the downward spiral that ofter follows a rapid rise to fame? Was she ever really (as she tells it) shy? In an intimate self-portrait, Lisa talks candidly about the key events that shaped her life choices in the crucible of childhood fame. -- back cover.
