This exciting, longer-length spin-off to Marton's miniseries The Barons finds attorney Gray Baron in Las Vegas to find Dawn Lincoln Kittredge, the long-lost grandchild of his uncle Jonas Baron. On the run from an abusive ex-husband, single mom Dawn is wary of strangers--even gorgeous ones like Gray. But mutual suspicion doesn't stop an undeniable passion from igniting between them. (July)
