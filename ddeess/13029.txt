A New York Times Bestselling Author Wade Benedict has a job to do: infiltrate a treacherous land and rescue Chloe Madison. It was her father's dying wish -- and Wade is taking it personally. The problem is, this stubborn, angry and courageous woman doesn't want to be saved, and no self-appointed rescuer can force her to abandon her friends or her commitments. But Chloe has met her match in Wade, a man as honorable and determined as she. Included only in Basic 7 Series.
