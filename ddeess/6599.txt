The search for the perfect man becomes a chilling nightmare in this sexy, suspense-filled blockbuster from the bestselling author of "All the Queen's Men". What qualities would make the perfect man? That's what four friends ponder in The List--a tongue-in-cheek checklist that they write on their lunch break. But when one of the women is murdered, a handsome police detective steps in to snare a killer that is targeting the three survivors, one by one.
