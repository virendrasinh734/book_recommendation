To Maggie Mayfair, the residents of her French Quarter building have become like family. When they hear the building is up for sale, Maggie decides to tap into the local folklore to scare away prospective buyers. Manhattan investment banker Nick Gerard plans to go undercover to straighten out the problems he's having trying to sell the apartment building, but he doesn't count on a stubborn group of tenants--or Maggie Mayfair.
