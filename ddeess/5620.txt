Smart and full of attitude, Josephine Fuller goes undercover as a troubleshooter for a feminist philanthropist. Working around the clock, it's a rare day that Josephine gets some time off. When she does, she heads to visit her friend Nina, a plus-size clothing designer. However, when Josephine gets to Seattle, Nina turns up dead. With a list of suspects that keeps growing, it's up to Josephine to find the killer before he strikes again.
