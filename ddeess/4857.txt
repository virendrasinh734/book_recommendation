When sisters Justine, Regina, and Mica Metcalf learn their parents are splitting up, they are reminded of how much they really have in common--like the local bad boy who proposed to Justine, seduced Regina, and ran off with Mica, and the murder they witnessed as girls and their vow to keep it secret. Soon, they discover how much they're willing to risk--and forgive--in the name of sisterhood. (July)
