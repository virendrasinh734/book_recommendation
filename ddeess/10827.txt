The anticipated sequel to "The Fifth Daughter" is set in 1820s Italy, after the fall of Napoleon and the Austrian Empire. Italian patriot Angelo Bartolini is a member of the Carbonari, a secret society dedicated to freeing Italy from Austrian rule. He soon becomes reacquainted with shy art student Beatrice Fairweather, who is also a Cabonari activist. Their attraction is instant but obstacles to their union abound. Original.
